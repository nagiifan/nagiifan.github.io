Overview

This catalog is packed with comfortable men's shirts in classic styles that were developed by the garment company I used to work for, as well as the company information. I designed this independently, then took it to the 2016 China Import and Export Fair for company promotion and product demonstration.


Company Introduction

Anhui Garments Import & Export Co. Ltd. is one of the largest foreign trade import and export companies in Anhui Province. It specializes in designing, developing, sourcing and manufacturing of garments, home textile, footwear, bags, suitcase, and home appliance, and offers international trade services worldwide.

Design Outcome

  • Garment Catalog


    This catalog successfully helped my department to attract new clients in China Import and Export Fair, which brought us orders value about a million dollars. It was then used as a catalog templet in the whole company.
