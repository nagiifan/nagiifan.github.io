Overview

This project is a Retail Management course project. We were required, in groups, to design a new fashion brand housed under a luxury conglomerate. The chief objective is to identify innovative ways to retain the existing customer base while also developing ideas on how to best attract new customers.

My role: I designed product packages.
Date: October-November 2018
Team: Cun, Jess, Kevin, Jimmy

Packages of Luxury Brands in Kering Group

According to my research, luxury brands in Kering group mostly use black and white as base colors in their package design. Package shapes are rectangle or square in general. To make our brand highly recognizable, at the same time coordinative with other brands in Kering, we need either change the colors or the shapes of our packages.


MoodBoards


Luxury streetwear uses the most popular elements in the trend, simple design but high-quality fabric/material and trim. Details display everywhere.

Design Decision

Concepts:

Brand attitude: Free / Rebel / Simple / Luxury
Industry trend: Sustainable
My interpretation: Unique shapes / Long-term use

Design Features:

  • Consistency: The apparel product package is designed into the shape of the letter O in our brand logo. The accessory package is designed into the shape of the logo crown. In this sense, we wish to present a high level of consistency, which can intensify customers' impression of our brand.

  • Distinct shape: Cylinder and tetrahedron shapes are not common in package design. We used this design to illustrate the uniqueness of our brand. Customers who buy our product and use our package will feel that they are special too.

  • Selected material: ROYL is a luxury brand that devotes to polishing detail. Therefore, we chose to use transparent material for the accessory package so that customers can see the delicate design of our products clearly from every angle.

  • Sustainability: Sustainability is the current trend in the fashion industry. To prove ROYL's high degree of social responsibility, all materials used for packages are clean and recyclable.
